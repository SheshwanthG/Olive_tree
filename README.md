# 🌿 The Olive Tree
### *A Visual Story by Sheshi (ss)*

> *"The world around us has many stories. The only thing is — we ignore them."*

---

## 📖 About

**The Olive Tree** is an interactive visual story set in the foothills of the West Himalayas, by the shores of the Ganga river.

A lonely writer, visited by a fairy named **Ren**, is told the forgotten story of the Olive Tree outside his window — a tale of love, loss, demons, magic, and a boy who loved so deeply he became a tree, his roots forever searching the river for the girl he lost.

---

## ✨ Characters

| Character | Role |

| **Sindri** | The girl taken by demons |
| **Ren** | The fairy who has lived through centuries |
| **Ramu** | Sindri's devoted father |
| *The Writer* | The lost storyteller who hears it all |

## 🎬 Experience

- 11 cinematic scroll scenes
- Hand-painted style character illustrations
- Animated rain, snow, fairy sparkles & river waves
- Typing effect on key story quotes
- Fully self-contained — works offline, no dependencies
